Understanding Mental Health

Mental health refers to our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how we think, feel, and behave, and helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.

Common Mental Health Issues

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Stress
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Bipolar Disorder

Tips for Maintaining Good Mental Health

  1. Exercise regularly to improve mood and reduce stress.
  2. Talk to someone you trust about your feelings.
  3. Get enough sleep and practice relaxation techniques.
  4. Eat a balanced diet and avoid harmful substances.
  5. Engage in activities that bring joy and satisfaction.
