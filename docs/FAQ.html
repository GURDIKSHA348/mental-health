Frequently Asked Questions

What is mental health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

How can I take care of my mental health?

You can take care of your mental health by getting enough sleep, eating well, exercising, and talking about your feelings.

Is therapy helpful for everyone?

Yes, therapy can help people understand themselves better and deal with life's challenges effectively.

Are my conversations confidential?

Yes, conversations with mental health professionals are private and kept confidential, unless there's a safety risk.

How can I contact a mental health professional?

You can contact a professional through our website contact form, email, or the provided phone number.
